## Technique Execution Results

### Technique 1: Role Playing (3 Personas)

**CTO Under Pressure** — Revealed: precision messaging, peer-to-peer voice, scarcity + low-friction CTA combo.
**Non-Technical Founder** — Revealed: business outcome translation, interim leadership positioning, CTO bridge partner concept, board-ready packaging.
**Enterprise Procurement Lead** — Revealed: compliance credentials, vendor documentation needs, certification checkboxes, anti-bait-and-switch positioning.

### Technique 2: SCAMPER (All 7 Lenses)

**Substitute** — Persona pathway, symptoms & prescriptions, war stories, proof of impact metrics.
**Combine** — Diagnose & Cure engine, direct-to-action flow, living enterprise CV.
**Adapt** — Productized consulting tiers, architecture firm aesthetic.
**Modify** — Real-time availability badge, interactive diagrams; vetoed animated counters for static devastating metrics.
**Put to Other Uses** — Tier links as proposals, dynamic persona sorting, gated toolkits.
**Eliminate** — Kill the "Our Team" page, "years of experience," blog archives, traditional nav bar (with safety-net footer caveat).
**Reverse/Rearrange** — "Mutual Fit" application, scaling friction self-assessment, anti-hero hero section, radical pricing transparency, reversed-risk guarantee.

### Technique 3: Analogical Thinking (4 Analogies)

**Michelin-Star Chef** → Premium aesthetic, scarcity psychology, architecture-diagram-as-hero-art.
**Emergency Room Triage** → No traditional homepage, "Where is the system bleeding?" immediate routing.
**High-End Real Estate Agent** → Quarterly "State of Nordic SaaS Architecture" authority reports.
**Special Forces Operator** → "Drop in, neutralize, train, extract." Anti-forever-consultant identity.

**Ultimate Brand DNA:** ER Triage UX × Michelin Aesthetic × Real Estate Authority × Special Forces Identity.
